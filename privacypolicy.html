Privacy Policy - PsicoLog

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Welcome to PsicoLog. Your privacy is the foundation of our architecture. We built this app on the principle that your thoughts, dreams, and emotions are strictly personal and must remain under your exclusive control.

1. "100% Offline" Architecture & Data Collection

PsicoLog operates with a total isolation philosophy. The app has no servers, uses no cloud services, and makes no internet connections for its core functionalities.

  • Personal Data: We do not collect, process, transmit, or sell any identifiable data (such as name, email, phone, IP, or location). The developer has no access to who you are or what you write.
  • User Content: All entries (journal, dreams, mood) are stored exclusively on your device's internal storage.
  • No Tracking: The app does not use cookies, tracking pixels, ads, or third-party analytics tools.

2. Security & Storage

We use your device's native security mechanisms to protect your data:

  • Data Isolation (App Sandboxing): The PsicoLog database is stored in the app's private directory, protected by the operating system's security (Android Sandbox or iOS Sandbox). This prevents other apps installed on your phone from reading your journals and dreams.
  • Access Control: We strongly recommend using the in-app "Biometric/Passcode Lock" feature to prevent people with physical access to your unlocked phone from opening PsicoLog.
  • User Responsibility: Using devices with modified security systems (such as "Root" or "Jailbreak") may compromise data isolation. It is the user's responsibility to maintain the integrity of the operating system.

3. Device Permissions

We request only permissions strictly necessary for local operation:

  • Biometrics (Fingerprint / Face ID): Used exclusively for the app lock screen feature. PsicoLog only receives an "Access Authorized" confirmation from the OS; we do not access or collect your raw biometric data.
  • Notifications: Used only to display therapy reminders or mood check-ins configured locally by you.

4. Backup & Data Export

You control your data. The app offers a manual export tool:

  • Backup File: You can generate a .json file containing your records. This file is generated in readable text so you can verify its content.
  • Backup Management: Safeguarding this exported file is your responsibility. If you save this file to cloud services or email it, the privacy policies of those respective services apply.

5. Data Deletion

Since we have no servers, we do not possess your data. To permanently delete your information, simply uninstall the app. Due to the Sandbox system, uninstallation permanently removes the database from your device storage.

6. Children's Privacy

PsicoLog is safe for all ages as it collects no data and displays no third-party content. We recommend that use by children under 13 be supervised by parents or guardians.
